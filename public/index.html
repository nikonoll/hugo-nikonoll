About Niko Noll

I'm currenlty finishing my Masters in Applied Computer Science at the University of Hagen while working part time on the Ruby team at Xing-Events. I'm currently also getting into Golang and ReactJS, ReduxJS and so on. I build Websites and WebApplications at Nolio | solutions.

Constantly learning and always finding new things to get passionate about. I think that is the most true and least douchy way to describe myself. Not that these characteristics don't come with there downsides. The urge to constantly explore new areas, subject, activies also leads to some level of superficiality in knowledge. But I just enjoy the dive into the unknown too much and I'm convinced that there is a need for generalists :)

In my time outside Uni/Work I run Trailruns and generally try to be as active as possible. Sometimes I help out at Refugees on Rails Munich and try to pass on some of the things I've learned about software development so far.
